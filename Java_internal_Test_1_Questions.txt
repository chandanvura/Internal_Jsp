Question 2 :
Write a program to print true if the Number given is Armstrong number otherwise
false.
An Armstrong number is a positive m-digit number that is equal to the sum of the rnth powers
of their digits. It is also known as pluperfect, or Plus Perfect, or Narcissistic number.
input-153
output-true
explanation : 13 + 53 + 33 = 1 + 125+ 27 = 153


input-125
output-false
explanation: 13 + 23 + 53 = 1 + 8 + 125 =134 (Not an Armstrong Number)
